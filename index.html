Popular destination

These are the places we visited and we shared our experiences.

  • Italy

    San miguel

    The Experience: San Miguel de Allende, a picturesque colonial town in central Mexico, is a feast for the senses. Its well-preserved historic center, characterized by cobblestone streets, colorful facades, and ornate churches, is a joy to explore.

  • Dubai

    Burj khalifa

    The Experience: The Burj Khalifa, an architectural marvel, stands as an emblem of Dubai's ambition and innovation. Soaring to dizzying heights, it's the world's tallest building, offering breathtaking views of the city and beyond.

  • Japan

    Kyoto temple

    Kyoto, a city steeped in tradition and elegance, is a captivating journey through Japan's rich cultural heritage. Its temples, with their ornate architecture and tranquil Zen gardens, are windows into a bygone era.

  • USA

    New york

    The Experience: New York City, the "Big Apple," is a global icon of culture, art, and diversity. The Statue of Liberty, Central Park, and Broadway shows are just the beginning. Explore eclectic neighborhoods, savor diverse cuisine, and be mesmerized by the city's skyscrapers.

  • italy

    Venice

    Venice, the "City of Canals," is a dreamy, waterborne wonder. Navigating the narrow canals in a gondola and marveling at historic architecture is an enchanting experience. Don't miss the St. Mark's Square, the Doge's Palace, and the vibrant Rialto Market.

  • japan

    Tokyo

    Tokyo is a vibrant metropolis where tradition meets modernity. From the serene Meiji Shrine to the bustling streets of Shibuya, Tokyo offers a diverse range of experiences.Enjoy world-class sushi, explore historic temples, and immerse yourself in the pop culture of Akihabara.
